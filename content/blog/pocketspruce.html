--- title: PocketSpruce kind: article created_at: 2013-11-25 --- To learn more about Ruby, I decided to start a project to scratch my own itch. I have been using Pocket for a while already, and collected many links, maybe even too many. I wanted a simple service which can email a daily random article from my list to me. Also, since there are many duplicate links and dead links in my list, I wanted the service to clean them up too. Introducing PocketSpruce.
